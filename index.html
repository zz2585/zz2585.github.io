Hi! I'm Zitong Zeng. I am a PhD candidate in accounting at UCLA Anderson School of Management. I am on the 2024-2025 job market.

My research investigates the economic consequences of accounting standards and corporate disclosures. I explore how financial reporting incentives influence firms' operational and disclosure decisions, and how these decisions in turn affect product market and capital market outcomes.

Prior to my doctoral studies at UCLA Anderson, I earned a B.B.A. in Management and Finance (with High Distinction) from University of Toronto and an M.A. in Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences from Columbia University.
